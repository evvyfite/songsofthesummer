Song Title

Please Please Please

Performer

Sabrina Carpenter

My Review

I like this song a little better than "Espresso." I still don't like her general singing style very much, but the lyrics of this song are very relatable, and she is a very talented lyricist and singer. It's a little jarring to hear her swear, but I honestly love that part, as a subversion of expectations is always fun. I still probably wouldn't listen to this on purpose, but I would enjoy hearing it play in Target.

My Rating

4 out of 5 stars.
